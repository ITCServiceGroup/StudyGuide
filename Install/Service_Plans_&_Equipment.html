300 Meg Plan

Authorized Equipment

ONTXGS or GPON based on Salesforce
RouterGoogle Wifi
ExtenderNone included.

Key Info & Eligibility

  • Status: Available reactively for qualifying addresses only. Not publicly advertised.
  • Price: $30/month.
  • Eligibility Check: Agents must use an internal dashboard to verify address eligibility.
  • Self-Verification Required: The customer must verbally confirm they meet income requirements (<$40k/year) and agree to provide documentation if requested. Agents must log this verification.
  • Once a customer leaves this plan, they cannot return unless they re-qualify at their address.

$0 Basic Plan

Authorized Equipment

ONTXGS or GPON based on Salesforce
RouterNone Provided (Customer Owned)
ExtenderNone Provided

Key Info & Eligibility

  • Status: Highly restricted availability.
  • Eligibility: Only available at a specific set of addresses in Kansas City, Provo, and Austin. Address eligibility expires over time.
  • Sign-up Process: Agents must verify address eligibility, then generate and send a custom signup URL to the customer.
  • No Downgrades: If a customer on this plan upgrades to any paid plan, they can never downgrade back to the Basic plan.
